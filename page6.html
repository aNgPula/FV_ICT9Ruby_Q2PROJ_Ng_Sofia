Champorado




Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cocoa Powder
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup glutinous/sticky rice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 12 fl. oz (1 can) evaporated milk

Instructions

  1. In a pot over high heat, put three (3) cups of water to boil. Then add glutinouse rice and let it cook for five (5) minutes, while stirring it from time to time.
  2. In warm water, let the cocoa powder dissolve in one (1) cup of warm water, making sure all the lumps are gone. Pour in the cocoa mixture in the pot of rice and cook it until it turns thick. After turning the heat to medium-low, add sugar and occasionally stir it to prevent the rice from sticking at the bottom. In case it gets too thick, add more water.
  3. Remove it from heat once the rice is done and the wanted consistency is achieved. (Note: The rice will still expand and absord the liquid after cooking.)
  4. Serve the Champorado in a bowl and add some evaporated milk on top, then done!
